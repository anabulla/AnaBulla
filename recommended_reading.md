# Recommended Reading

A list of books and articles that influenced my thinking in statistics, data visualization, and R.

### Books

- **[Storytelling with Data](https://www.amazon.com/Storytelling-Visualizacao-Profissionais-Negocios-Portugues/dp/8550804681)** — Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic  
  Great for learning how to communicate data clearly and effectively.

- **[R for Data Science](https://pt.r4ds.hadley.nz/#sobre-a-versão-original)** — Hadley Wickham, Mine Çetinkaya-Rundel, Garrett Grolemund  
  A practical introduction to data analysis and visualization in R.

- **[O andar do bêbado: Como o acaso determina nossas vidas]()** — Leonard Mlodinow
